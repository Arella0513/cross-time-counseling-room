Classroom Thinking Task

After listening to the two poets, compare their ideas about returning to nature.

Tao Yuanming: nature as a peaceful home for spiritual independence.

Thoreau: nature as a place for deliberate living and self-discovery.

Guiding Question: Is returning to nature an escape from reality, or a wiser way to face life?
Returning to nature is not escaping from life, but finding a clearer and more peaceful way to live.
